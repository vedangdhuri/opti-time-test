About the Institute

Yashwantrao Bhonsale Institute of Technology, Sawantwadi is a reputed technical institute committed to delivering quality education in the field of engineering and technology. The institute focuses on academic excellence, practical learning, and industry-oriented skill development.

The curriculum is designed to bridge the gap between theoretical concepts and real-world applications, preparing students for professional careers and higher education.

About the Project

The College Timetable Management System is a web-based application developed using Django, SQLite, and Bootstrap. It automates the generation, management, and visualization of academic timetables.

  • Automatic timetable generation
  • Admin-controlled data management
  • Batch-wise and day-wise scheduling
  • Analytics dashboard for insights

This system reduces manual effort, avoids scheduling conflicts, and improves overall academic planning efficiency.
